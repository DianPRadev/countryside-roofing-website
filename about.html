Who We Are

More Than 50 Years of Protecting Chicagoland Homes

Countryside Roofing, Siding and Windows was founded in 1973 with a simple belief: do excellent work, treat customers honestly, and the business will take care of itself. Five decades later, that philosophy still guides everything we do.

We are a true family-owned and operated company. When you call Countryside, you're not reaching a call center — you're talking to people who live and work in the same communities you do. Our team of field representatives, project managers, and installation crews have decades of combined experience and take genuine pride in every project they touch.

Our reputation speaks for itself: over 65% of our new customers come to us through referrals from happy neighbors, friends, and family members. That's not a marketing metric — it's the result of doing honest, high-quality work year after year.

How We Operate

The Principles Behind Every Project

We don't just install roofs and siding. We build relationships — and these are the values that guide every interaction.

01
Honest Consultation
We will always recommend what your home actually needs — even if that means suggesting a repair instead of a full replacement. Your long-term best interest comes before a bigger sale.
02
Uncompromising Quality
We only use materials from trusted, reputable manufacturers, and our installation standards exceed industry norms. A job not done right is a job not done.
03
Clear Communication
From the initial estimate to the final walk-through, you'll always know what's happening, when it's happening, and why. No surprises. No runaround.
04
Respect for Your Property
Our crews treat your home like it's their own — using magnetic rollers to collect nails, daily clean-up, and careful protection of landscaping and driveways.
05
We Stand Behind Our Work
Every project is backed by a lifetime manufacturer material warranty and our own 5-year labor warranty. If something isn't right, we make it right.
06
Community First
We've served the same Chicagoland communities for over 50 years. Our neighbors' trust is the foundation everything else is built on — and we never take it for granted.
