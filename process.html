Before We Arrive

How to Prepare for Your Project Day

A little preparation makes the day go even smoother. Here's what we recommend.

Roofing Projects
  • Clear vehicles from the driveway
  • Move patio furniture away from the house
  • Remove wall hangings or fragile items from interior walls
  • Give us access to your backyard gate if needed
  • Let neighbors know in advance about noise
Siding Projects
  • Clear a 4-foot perimeter around your home
  • Remove outdoor decorations and light fixtures
  • Trim any overgrown shrubs near the walls
  • Ensure exterior outlets are accessible
  • Secure any outdoor pets away from work areas
Window Projects
  • Remove window treatments and blinds
  • Clear furniture 3 feet away from each window
  • Remove any window A/C units in advance
  • Clear exterior window sills and flower boxes
  • Ensure all windows to be replaced are accessible
Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does a roof replacement take? +
Most residential roof replacements are completed in a single day. Larger or more complex roofs may take two days. We provide a specific timeline in your project proposal before any work begins.
Do I need to be home during the installation? +
You don't need to be home for the duration of the project, but we recommend being available at the start of the day when the crew arrives, and at the end for the final walk-through. Your rep will be in contact throughout the day.
What happens if you find unexpected damage during the project? +
If we discover rotted wood, damaged decking, or other issues during tear-off, we stop and contact you before proceeding. We explain what we found, what it means, and the cost to address it. You approve any changes before work continues.
Do you use subcontractors? +
No. All installation work is performed by our own trained, experienced crews — not subcontractors. This is one of the reasons we can stand behind our 5-year labor warranty with confidence.
Can you help me with my insurance claim? +
Absolutely. We have extensive experience working with insurance adjusters and can assist throughout the entire claims process — from initial documentation to final approval. Many of our customers have found we're able to help them receive more comprehensive coverage than they expected.
What warranties do you offer? +
Every project includes two layers of warranty protection: a lifetime manufacturer material warranty on all products we install, and our own 5-year labor warranty covering the quality of our installation work. Details are outlined in every project contract.
Do you offer financing? +
Yes, financing options are available. Every homeowner's situation is unique, so we encourage you to call us at (847) 221-5600 to discuss which options might work best for your project and budget.
